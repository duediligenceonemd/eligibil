AI Funding Intelligence for Startup Founders

Eligibil.org matches startup founders with 700+ verified grants, accelerators and non-dilutive capital programs across Moldova, Romania, the EU and globally. AI-powered eligibility scoring, document analysis and deadline tracking.

What we do

  • Funding discovery — searchable database of 700+ grants, accelerators and non-dilutive programs across 15+ countries
  • AI eligibility matching — semantic comparison between your startup profile and funding program criteria
  • Document analysis — AI scoring of pitch decks, whitepapers and videos against funding requirements
  • Deadline tracking — alerts and calendar for upcoming application deadlines
  • Multi-language — full interface in Romanian, English, Russian and Ukrainian

Who it's for

Startup founders in Moldova, Romania and the EU seeking non-dilutive funding — grants, accelerator programs, public tenders and ecosystem resources.

Status

Eligibil.org is currently in early access development (MVP stage). Built by AIGOV SOLUTION SRL, Republic of Moldova. Founder: Stanislav Florica.
